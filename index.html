Why RJ Tutors
B · approach

Personal, not industrial.

Tutoring built on rapport, real experience, and tools that fit how children actually learn.

  1. 01

    One student at a time.

    Each session is built from what your child needs that week, not a fixed curriculum dragged forward at the same pace for everyone.

    ·
  2. 02

    Ideas before answers.

    We teach the bigger concept first, the why behind the question. Once a student sees how something actually works, the right answer tends to follow on its own.

    ·
  3. 03

    Tutors who share what they know.

    The tutors at RJ bring their own academic and working experience into lessons where it helps, so subjects connect to actual life instead of sitting flat on a page. Trust and rapport build from there.

    ·
  4. 04

    Tools that match how children learn.

    Sessions run on Microsoft Teams with live iPad notes your child can watch being written. Between lessons, students get digital textbooks and recommended resources to keep working with.

    ·
  5. 05

    Confidence, not just grades.

    Tutoring should leave a student more capable in themselves, not just better-prepared for one exam. Confidence, character, and grades build together.

    ·

How it works
C · process

From first call to first lesson, in days. Not weeks.

A short, careful start so the first session is genuinely useful for your child.

  1. i

    Tell us about your child

    A short call about current level, exam timing, and how things are going at school right now.

  2. ii

    We pair you with a tutor

    Matched on subject, exam board, and personality. One suggestion at a time, never a list to sift through.

  3. iii

    First lesson, free

    A real session over Microsoft Teams. You and your child meet the tutor and see how it works before deciding anything.

  4. iv

    Weekly sessions, tracked simply

    Live iPad notes during the lesson, digital resources between, and a short progress note so you know where things stand.
